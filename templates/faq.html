Q. How can I improve my Listicles

A.

- Number each item

- "Top, Best, Most": Make your list title the most it can be, example: 8 Top Reasons to Make Extra money, 12 Mistakes Most Posters Make. etc...

- Give your list a Great (but short) title.

- Be controversial: Topics that get attention get more shares on social media.

- Motivate, inspire and teach. Lists that motivate people perform really well and people enjoy sharing things that inspire them.

- Be topical: Use a subject that is really big at the moment.

- Mention famous people, they tend to get alot of attention.
